## The New Soul of the South

Ahangama isn't just a town; it's a vibe. It has replaced Unawatuna and Hikkaduwa as the destination for the modern traveler.

### Where to Eat
- **Trax**: Garden cafe with great coffee.
- **Cactus**: Vegan friendly, sunset views.
- **Crust**: Best pizza in the south.

### Surf Breaks
- **The Rock**: For intermediate/advanced.
- **Sticks**: Long rides, fun vibe.
- **Marshmallow**: Gentle waves for beginners.
