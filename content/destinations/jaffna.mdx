## The Undiscovered North

Jaffna feels like a different country. The language is Tamil, the food is spicy, and the hospitality is legendary.

### Must See
- **Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil**: The spiritual center.
- **Jaffna Fort**: Dutch colonial architecture.
- **Rio Ice Cream**: A local institution.
