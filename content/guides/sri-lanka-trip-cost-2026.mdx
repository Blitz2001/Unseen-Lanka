## The Short Answer

**Budget**: $30-50/day per person  
**Mid-Range**: $100-150/day per person  
**Luxury**: $300+/day per person

But let's break it down properly.

## Accommodation Costs

### Budget ($10-25/night)
- Hostels: $10-15/night (dorm bed)
- Guesthouses: $20-25/night (private room)
- **Where**: Ella, Arugam Bay, Negombo

### Mid-Range ($50-100/night)
- Boutique hotels: $60-80/night
- Beach resorts: $70-100/night
- **Where**: Galle, Mirissa, Kandy

### Luxury ($150-500+/night)
- 5-star resorts: $200-350/night
- Private villas: $300-500+/night
- **Where**: Tangalle, Bentota, Sigiriya

## Food & Drink

### Budget ($5-10/day)
- Rice & curry at local spots: $2-3/meal
- Street food: $1-2/snack
- Bottled water: $0.50

### Mid-Range ($20-30/day)
- Tourist restaurant meals: $8-12/meal
- Cafe breakfast: $5-7
- Beer: $3-4

### Luxury ($50+/day)
- Fine dining: $30-50/meal
- Hotel breakfast buffet: $15-20
- Cocktails: $8-12

## Transport

### Trains
- 2nd class: $1-5 (long distances)
- 1st class: $3-10
- **Example**: Colombo to Ella = $5 (2nd class)

### Buses
- Local buses: $0.50-2
- Air-conditioned buses: $3-8

### Private Driver
- Full day (8 hours): $50-80
- Airport transfer: $30-50
- **Recommended for**: Families, groups, comfort

### Tuk-Tuks
- Short ride (5km): $3-5
- Negotiate before getting in

## Activities & Entrance Fees

### Paid Attractions
- Sigiriya Rock: $30
- Temple of the Tooth (Kandy): $10
- Yala Safari: $40-60
- Whale watching: $40-50

### Free Activities
- Beaches (all of them)
- Hiking (Little Adam's Peak, Ella Rock)
- Temple visits (most)

## Money-Saving Tips

1. **Travel Off-Season** (May-September): 30-40% cheaper accommodation
2. **Eat Local**: Rice & curry is delicious and costs $2
3. **Use Trains**: Scenic and dirt cheap
4. **Negotiate**: Tuk-tuks, guesthouses, markets
5. **Book Direct**: Hotels often offer better rates than booking sites

## What's Expensive in Sri Lanka?

- **Alcohol**: Heavily taxed. Beer is $3-4, wine is $15+
- **Tourist Attractions**: Sigiriya, Yala, etc. have high foreigner prices
- **Western Food**: Pizza, pasta, burgers cost 3x local food

## What's Cheap?

- **Public Transport**: Trains and buses are absurdly cheap
- **Local Food**: Rice & curry, kottu, hoppers
- **Beaches**: All free, no entrance fees
