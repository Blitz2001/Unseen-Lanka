## Do You Need a Visa for Sri Lanka?

Most travelers need an **Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA)** before arriving in Sri Lanka. Here's everything you need to know for 2026.

## Who Needs an ETA?

**Almost everyone.** Citizens of 190+ countries require an ETA, including:
- United States
- United Kingdom
- European Union
- Australia
- Canada
- India
- China

### Visa-Free Countries (2026)

Only **Singapore, Maldives, and Seychelles** citizens can enter visa-free for up to 30 days.

## How to Apply for Sri Lanka ETA

### Online Application (Recommended)

1. Visit the official ETA website: [www.eta.gov.lk](https://www.eta.gov.lk)
2. Fill out the application form (5-10 minutes)
3. Pay the fee online (credit/debit card)
4. Receive ETA approval via email (usually within 24 hours)

### On Arrival (Not Recommended)

You can apply at Colombo Airport, but expect:
- Long queues (30-60 minutes)
- Higher fees
- Potential delays if there are issues

**Pro Tip**: Apply online at least 3 days before travel.

## ETA Fees (2026)

- **Tourist ETA (30 days)**: $50 USD
- **Business ETA (30 days)**: $100 USD
- **Transit ETA (48 hours)**: $25 USD

Payment methods: Visa, Mastercard, American Express.

## ETA Validity

- **Single Entry**: Valid for 30 days from arrival
- **Multiple Entry**: Available for business visas only
- **Extension**: Can be extended up to 6 months at the Department of Immigration in Colombo

## What You Need to Apply

- Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
- Return flight ticket
- Proof of accommodation (hotel booking or invitation letter)
- Credit/debit card for payment

## Arrival Process

1. **Immigration**: Show your passport and ETA approval email (print or digital)
2. **Stamp**: You'll receive a 30-day tourist stamp
3. **Customs**: Declare any restricted items

**Average processing time at immigration**: 10-20 minutes.

## Common Issues & Solutions

### "My ETA was rejected"
- Check passport validity (must be 6+ months)
- Ensure all details match your passport exactly
- Contact the ETA helpline: +94 11 2377 777

### "I made a mistake on my application"
- You cannot edit after submission
- Apply again with correct details (you'll be charged again)

### "I lost my ETA approval email"
- Check your spam folder
- Contact ETA support with your passport number

## Extensions

To extend beyond 30 days:
1. Visit the Department of Immigration in Colombo
2. Bring passport, ETA approval, proof of funds, and return ticket
3. Fee: ~$100 USD per month extension
4. Processing time: 1-2 weeks

## Special Cases

### Indian Citizens
Free ETA for tourism (30 days). Apply online before travel.

### Children
All travelers, including infants, need an ETA.

### Dual Citizenship
Use the passport you'll travel with for the ETA application.

## Important Notes

- ETA does not guarantee entry. Immigration officers have final say.
- Overstaying your visa results in fines ($100+ per day) and potential deportation.
- Working on a tourist ETA is illegal.
