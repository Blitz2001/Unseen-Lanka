## Why This Journey?

For decades, the North was closed. Now, it is the last frontier of authentic Sri Lankan culture. This is not a tour for those who want to tick off boxes. It is for those who want to understand the soul of a resilience nation.

### Highlights
- **Jaffna**: Explore the Nallur Kovil and tasting authentic crab curry.
- **Delft Island**: Meet the wild ponies on this remote limestone island.
- **Trincomalee**: Whale watching and temple visits on the East Coast.
- **Ritigala**: Walk through the ancient monastic ruins in the jungle.

## Itinerary Overview

**Day 1-3: The Northern Capital**
Arrival in Colombo and immediate transfer to Anuradhapura. Next day, the train to Jaffna.

**Day 4-5: Islands & history**
Boat ride to Delft Island. Exploring the Jaffna Fort.

**Day 6-9: The East Coast**
Transfer to Trincomalee. Beach time, Koneswaram Temple, and Pigeon Island snorkeling.

**Day 10-12: The Cultural Triangle & Departure**
Ritigala ruins, Sigiriya (optional), and return to Colombo.
