## The Vibe

The South Coast is where Sri Lanka relaxes. It's a blend of colonial history in Galle, hipster surf culture in Ahangama, and raw wilderness in Yala.

### Highlights
- **Galle Fort**: Walk the ramparts at sunset.
- **Ahangama**: Surf lessons and cafe hopping.
- **Yala National Park**: Leopard safari.
- **Mirissa**: Blue whale watching.

## Itinerary Overview

**Day 1-3: Galle Fort**
Stay inside the UNESCO heritage fort. Boutique shopping and history.

**Day 4-7: The Surf Belt (Ahangama)**
Slow days. Morning surf, afternoon yoga, evening seafood.

**Day 8-10: Wild Yala**
Transfer to Yala. Jeep safari. Glamping under the stars.
